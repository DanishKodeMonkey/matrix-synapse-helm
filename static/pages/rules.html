Server Rules

These rules apply to all rooms and direct messages on this homeserver. Enforcement is at moderator discretion.

1. Respect other users

  • No harassment, hate speech, intimidation, or personal attacks.
  • No doxxing or sharing private information without consent.

2. Keep content legal and safe

  • No illegal content or malicious links/files.
  • No spam, scams, impersonation, or automated abuse.

3. Room hygiene

  • Stay on topic in topic-specific rooms.
  • Use clear room names and descriptions when creating rooms/spaces.
  • Public room listing requires admin approval.

4. Moderation actions

  • Actions include warning, mute, kick, ban, and account suspension.
  • Severe abuse may be escalated immediately without warning.

5. Appeals

  • Contact admins in the support/mod room for appeals.
  • Provide room, date, and brief context for review.
